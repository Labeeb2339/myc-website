What Are MCreds?

MCreds are our participation credits. Show up, help out, create something — earn MCreds. Trade them for perks: early access, limited merch, stage time & more.

  • Earn: attend events, volunteer, submit projects
  • Level up: stack MCreds to unlock tiers
  • Redeem: swap for perks, workshops, collab slots

Season 1 Perks

  • Exclusive neon sticker pack
  • Workshop priority seating
  • Feature on MYC socials

FAQ

When does the Join button unlock?

At local midnight on January 1 (your device time). When the countdown hits zero, the button glows and unlocks.

Do I need an account?

On Jan 1, clicking Join opens a simple form. You can add your name, school, and interests — no account required.

Where are events hosted?

Miri, with occasional collabs across Sarawak. Locations appear on each event card.
